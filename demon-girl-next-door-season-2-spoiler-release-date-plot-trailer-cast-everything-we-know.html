Storyline

The Demon Girl Next Door is a comedy and slice-of-life anime that follows the adventures of Yuko Yoshida, a descendant of the Dark Clan that was cursed by the Light Clan. One day, she wakes up with horns and a tail and learns that she has to defeat a magical girl to break the curse and restore her clan’s honor. However, the magical girl turns out to be Momo Chiyoda, a kind and friendly girl who attends the same school as Yuko. Instead of fighting, Momo decides to help Yuko train and improve her skills, while also becoming her friend and confidant.
