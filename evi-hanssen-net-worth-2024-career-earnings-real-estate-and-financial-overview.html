Evi Hanssen Finance Overview

Early Career and Initial Earnings

Evi Hanssen‘s journey to financial success began with her role as a background singer and actor in the Samson en Gert Christmas shows. Playing Sofie, the niece of the mayor, at the young age of 16, Hanssen laid the groundwork for her future endeavors.

These early opportunities in entertainment were not just stepping stones but pivotal moments that contributed to her initial earnings and growing public presence.

Breakthrough as a Television Presenter

Hanssen’s major breakthrough came in April 2001 when she became a VJ on TMF Flanders. Her role involved presenting alongside Elke Vanelderen in the show Boobietrap and covering major festivals like Marktrock, Suikerrock, TW Classic, and I Love Techno.

These high-profile assignments significantly boosted her earnings and solidified her status as a prominent television personality.

Music Career and Financial Impact

In music, Hanssen formed the group Hookers Green and released the debut single Down as she goes. This track, which became a favorite of Greenpeace Belgium, marked a significant milestone in her career.

Earnings from Television and Media Career

Evi Hanssen’s career in television expanded further with her roles on shows like De Farm, 71° Noord, and Expeditie Robinson, which she presented annually from 2006 to 2013. She was also a regular on shows like De Foute Quiz and GodzijDank.

In 2008 and 2009, Hanssen hosted the Flemish version of Project Runway, known as De Designers. Each of these roles contributed to her growing net worth through consistent television presence and viewer engagement.

Voice Acting and Other Media Ventures

Hanssen also ventured into voice acting, dubbing the voice of Garfield in Garfield: The Movie. Her work as a flying reporter for Radio Donna and appearances on the sports channel Sporza further diversified her income.

These roles highlight her versatility and ability to adapt to various facets of the entertainment industry, thereby enhancing her financial standing.

Business Ventures and Production House

Hanssen co-founded the production house Bonhanssa with her ex-husband Fred di Bono. This venture, along with other business initiatives, played a crucial role in her financial growth.

The production house allowed Hanssen to leverage her industry experience and expand her influence beyond performance roles, adding a substantial income stream to her portfolio.

Public Appearances and Endorsements

Public appearances and interviews with celebrities like Lenny Kravitz and Justin Timberlake boosted Hanssen’s profile.

These engagements, coupled with endorsements, added to her revenue, showcasing the financial benefits of maintaining a strong public image.
