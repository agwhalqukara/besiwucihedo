Real Madrid: Luka Modric renews at Real Madrid until 2022

September 2024 Â· 1 minute read

Luka Modric has extended his Real Madrid contract until June 30, 2022.

The agreement, as reported by MARCA, had been agreed three months before the end of the season but the club were waiting until the end of the season to announce it publicly.

Modric has signed a one-year extension, conforming to the club's policy of only offering players over 30 one-year deals.

Playing 48 games in all competitions during 2020/21, Modric was one of Los Blancos' most important players, accumulating 3,721 minutes.

More than happy

"Happy and proud to continue wearing the shirt of the best club in the world," Modric said in a post on social media.

Now, the Croatian hopes to play at the renovated Estadio Santiago Bernabeu and to finish his career at Real Madrid.
