'Up In The Blue Seats' Podcast episode 144: Igor Shesterkin rebounds

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Have no fear Rangers fans, Igor Shesterkin looks back to his old ways.

The Rangers goalie shut out the Flames on Tuesday after defeating the Blackhawks last Friday.

The Blueshirts are on a five-game win streak and in first place.

The vibes are good in Rangers land with a little more than three weeks until the trade deadline.

First up, it’s the Stadium Series on Sunday at MetLife Stadium.

Brian Boyle knows a thing or two about the Stadium Series in a Rangers uniform.

Boyle, Larry Brooks and Andrew Harts talk everything Stadium Series, Rangers and where the team stands on a new episode of the “Up In The Blue Seats” podcast.

Up In The Blue Seats Podcast with Brian Boyle, Larry Brooks & Andrew Harts:

  • IGOR’S BACK: Shesterkin is back looking good. He has quieted the noise. He played with some swagger back.
  • WHAT NEEDS TO CHANGE: Getting them to understand that the time is right now. Some guys just need to improve and play to the levels they have played to in the past.
  • STADIUM SERIES: Not a ton of talk within the team about it. Guys brought it up Wednesday. Looking back at some of the past stadium series games.
  • YANKEE STADIUM STADIUM SERIES: Boyle looks back to the 2014 Stadium Series at Yankee Stadium and his memories from the game.
  • BOYLE-ING POINT: Boyle vents his frustrations on school getting canceled and snow days.
